About Me

Hello, I’m Ms. Nirojai Sritharan an educator, entrepreneur, and founder of MIWIE.


With a strong foundation in Early Childhood Education and years of teaching experience, I bring creativity, discipline, and purpose to everything I do. My journey began in the classroom, where I taught English to primary and secondary students for over four years. Today, I’m pursuing my Bachelor of Arts in Education while building my creative brand from the ground up.


In 2021, I launched MIWIE, a unique handmade crafts brand inspired by imagination and emotion. From elegant keychains and coasters to custom shadow boxes and resin art, each creation tells a story – designed with care and crafted with heart.


Driven by passion and purpose, I balance education and entrepreneurship to bring meaningful, memorable pieces to life. Whether I’m in the studio or studying, my mission remains the same: to inspire through creativity and connect through craft.


Let’s create something unforgettable, together.
